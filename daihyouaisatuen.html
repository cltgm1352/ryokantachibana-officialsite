Message from the Owner

In the mountain village of Urakawa, Hamamatsu, I run a small inn where guests can feel the beauty of nature and the warmth of the local community.

With the wish to offer “a moment of peace and a truly local experience to each guest,” I started this inn by revitalizing a vacant house.

As our region faces challenges such as depopulation and vacant homes, I hope that welcoming travelers will help preserve local culture and connect it to the future.

Please relax and enjoy your time here.

Ryokan Tachibana Representative Yuta Naito
